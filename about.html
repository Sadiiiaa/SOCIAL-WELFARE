About Our Organization

For over 25 years, we've been dedicated to serving our community with compassion, dignity, and unwavering commitment.

Our Story & Mission

The Social Welfare Department was established in 1999 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide comprehensive support and assistance to the most vulnerable members of our community. What started as a small team of passionate volunteers has grown into a full-fledged organization serving thousands of families annually.

Our approach is rooted in the belief that everyone deserves access to basic necessities, dignity, and the opportunity to thrive. We work tirelessly to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting positive change in our community.

Through our diverse programs and services, we address immediate needs while also implementing long-term solutions that empower individuals and families to achieve self-sufficiency.
