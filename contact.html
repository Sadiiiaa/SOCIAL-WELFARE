Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and how to get help.

You can apply for assistance by calling our main office, visiting any of our service centers, or filling out the contact form above. Our team will guide you through the application process and help determine which programs you're eligible for.

Required documents typically include: valid ID, proof of income, proof of residence, and Social Security numbers for all household members. However, if you don't have all documents, please contact us anyway - we can help you obtain what you need.

We welcome volunteers! You can start by filling out the contact form above and selecting "Volunteer Information" as the subject. Our volunteer coordinator will contact you to discuss available opportunities and schedule an orientation.

Yes, our emergency hotline 1-800-HELP-NOW is available 24/7 for crisis situations and urgent assistance needs. For non-emergency inquiries, please contact us during regular business hours.
