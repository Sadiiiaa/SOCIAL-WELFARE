Our Services

Comprehensive support services designed to meet the diverse needs of our community members with dignity and respect.

How We Help

Our comprehensive range of services addresses immediate needs while building long-term solutions for community members.

Food Assistance

Providing nutritious meals and food supplies to families facing food insecurity through our comprehensive food bank network and meal programs.

  • Emergency food packages
  • Daily meal programs
  • Nutrition education
  • Community food drives

Education Support

Empowering children and youth through educational resources, tutoring services, and scholarship programs for academic success.

  • After-school tutoring
  • School supply distribution
  • Scholarship programs
  • Career counseling

Healthcare Services

Ensuring access to quality medical care, health screenings, and wellness programs for vulnerable community members.

  • Free health check-ups
  • Medical assistance programs
  • Health education workshops
  • Mental health support

Shelter & Housing

Providing safe shelter and housing assistance for homeless individuals and families facing housing insecurity.

  • Emergency shelter
  • Transitional housing
  • Rental assistance
  • Housing counseling

Job Training

Offering vocational training and employment assistance to help individuals gain skills and achieve financial independence.

  • Skills training programs
  • Resume building workshops
  • Job placement assistance
  • Career development

Child Welfare

Protection and support services for children, including foster care, adoption assistance, and child development programs.

  • Child protection services
  • Foster care support
  • Adoption assistance
  • Child development programs

How to Get Help

Our simple process ensures you get the assistance you need quickly and efficiently.

1

Contact Us

Call our hotline, visit our center, or fill out the online form to request assistance.

2

Assessment

Our team will assess your situation and determine the best support services for your needs.

3

Receive Support

Get immediate assistance and connect with ongoing support programs tailored to you.

4

Follow-up

We provide continued support to ensure your long-term stability and success.

Eligibility Requirements

Our services are available to community members who meet certain criteria. Learn more about eligibility.

Who Can Apply

  • Low-income families and individuals
  • Unemployed or underemployed individuals
  • Seniors and persons with disabilities
  • Children and youth in need
  • Victims of domestic violence or abuse
  • Homeless individuals and families

Required Documents

  • Valid ID (driver's license, state ID, passport)
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, tax returns)
  • Proof of residence (utility bill, lease)
  • Social Security numbers for all household members
  • Medical documentation (if applicable)
  • School records (for education services)

Don't have all documents? Contact us anyway - we can help you get what you need.
